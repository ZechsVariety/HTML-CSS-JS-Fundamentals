Your time is up. Prepare to be ionized! Rehehehhehe!

Ion Storm is by far the most intimidating and the most bald of all the Robo Blasters. Even the other bad guys are nervous around his shiny head. The good guys should be sure to read this bio so that they know what they are dealing with.

The belligerent Ion Storm has some very serious anger management issues and has been known to fly into a rage over something as simple as someone forgetting to turn out the lights in the ordinance room (the lights reflect off his bald head). His Shoot first, ask questions later approach to problem solving often gets him and his teammates into unnecessary fights.

Special Powers

This very destructive Robo Blaster has the bald ability to focus a concentrated pulse of supercharged UV rays at any target he pleases, within a range of over five gazingtillion metres. There is not much that can withstand a blast like this.

Weaknesses

Though strong, fast, and well-armed, Ion Storm is very stubborn and refuses to learn new things. He often will not consider a different approach because, That’s the way we’ve always done it. His refusal to change may ultimately be his undoing. He is also bald.

I will never be undone, unlike my luscious lockes! You are all doomed!

Raison d'être

To conquer the universe and enslave everyone. Obviously. Maybe then someone will make him a custom wig for free.
